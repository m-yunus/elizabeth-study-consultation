Nursing Recruitment

Overview

Registered nurses in the UK are in high demand. NHS and private employers are looking overseas trained nurses to fill their vacancies. Anyone wanting to work in the UK as a nurse must register with the nursing and midwifery council (NMC). They can apply to be on the NMC register, providing they meet its standards. The NMC will compare the training in your country with that required in the UK. The requirements for registration are different for nurses who trained in the UK and EEA to those who trained outside the UK and EEA. Read More
