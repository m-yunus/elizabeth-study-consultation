FAQ

1What is Elizabeth International?

Elizabeth International is a pioneer in recruiting nurses overseas. In the field for the past 20+ years Elizabeth International is even equipped with our language training institute and career guidance programs.

2Why chose Elizabeth International?

Elizabeth international’s primary concern is their candidate and are vowed to help them in every possible way to make their dream of working as a nurse in the UK come true. We pride ourselves in providing exceptional personal guidance along each and every step of the process.

3How do I get started?

We have made the recruitment as easy as possible. Send in your CV to our email or drop a message on our whatsapp number. Our advisors will guide you through calls, video calls or in person (UK only).

4 Do you provide support after coming to UK?

We provide support for the candidates OSCE test after coming to UK. Our office are open for our candidates for any assist.

Elizabeth International is a committed group of people who are confident in providing high-quality services to the healthcare sector.
