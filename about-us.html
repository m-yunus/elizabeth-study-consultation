About us

With humble beginnings in Kerala, India, Elizabeth International has now grown into one of the most trusted overseas nursing recruiter in Kerala. Due to the huge demand in nursing for the United Kingdom, we have now expanded overseas to meet the demand for the UK, Ireland, Australia and New Zealand. ​ What separates us from the rest in the industry is the exceptional and flexibility in providing one-on-one guidance.
